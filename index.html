Gameplay

  • The Court is 9 x 9m at every site
  • The Net is between 2,24-2,43 m high
  • Player may not hit the ball twice in succession
  • You get a point if the ball lands in the field, of the other team
  • 6 players on a team, 3 on the front row and 3 on the back row.

Sets

  • A team wins if they are the winner of 3 sets
  • The winner of a set needs at Least to 25 points and two more than the other team
  • The Fifth set (if needed) has just 15 points
